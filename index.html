What's Inside

Inside the Can

Four proprietary technologies working together to stop fires faster than anything else

Proprietary Patented
Compound Formula

At the heart of HALT's fire extinguisher is our proprietary, patented compound formula that brings together the latest advancements in chemical fire suppression. This innovative blend is crafted to tackle flames faster and more effectively than traditional methods. The formula combines unique fire-retardant chemicals that react quickly to snuff out fires by attacking all three critical elements: heat, fuel, and oxygen.

Class K — Grease Fire
Stopped in seconds. No mess. No toxins.

Lineman
Technology

Originating from technology developed for electrical linemen working in high-risk environments, our Lineman Technology is designed to function under extreme conditions. These professionals require fast-acting, reliable fire suppression solutions. HALT leverages this background to deliver a one-hand operable extinguisher that activates rapidly, effectively managing fire emergencies with precision and urgency.

One-Hand Operation

No training required. Designed for instant use under pressure.

Extreme Conditions

Proven performance in the most demanding environments.

Glassy Boron
Agents

A critical ingredient in our extinguishing formula is Glassy Boron, an innovative substance known for its ability to smother and seal fuel sources from oxygen. When deployed, Glassy Boron creates a non-flammable barrier over the fuel, effectively preventing the fire from spreading. This chemical barrier not only suppresses existing flames but also prevents re-ignition by isolating the fuel from potential sparks or heat sources.

0%

Reignition Rate

Glassy Boron seals fuel sources from oxygen — permanently stopping fire at its source.

Leidenfrost
Effect

HALT's approach to combating intense heat involves a clever application of the Leidenfrost Effect, where our specially formulated wetting agents are designed to penetrate the steam layer that fire generates. This allows the agent to cool the burning material more efficiently than water or traditional foam. By breaking through this thermal barrier, our extinguisher rapidly cools down the fire's core temperature.

Penetrates Steam Layer

Cuts through thermal barriers that stop traditional suppressants.

Rapid Core Cooling

Significantly reduces chance of flare-ups for a faster resolution.
